## What is AntiQuantum Wallet?

AntiQuantum Wallet is the world's first **quantum-resistant Solana wallet** — built to protect your crypto assets before quantum computers become powerful enough to break classical cryptography (Ed25519, ECDSA).

Most crypto wallets today use algorithms that **quantum computers will be able to crack**. AntiQuantum Wallet solves this by implementing **NIST FIPS 204 (ML-DSA-65)** for signing and **NIST FIPS 203 (ML-KEM-1024)** for encryption — the gold standard in post-quantum cryptography.

## Why Post-Quantum Now?

Quantum computers running **Shor's algorithm** can break:
- ❌ Bitcoin's secp256k1 (ECDSA)
- ❌ Ethereum's secp256k1 (ECDSA)
- ❌ Solana's Ed25519

AntiQuantum replaces these with lattice-based cryptography that quantum computers **cannot break**, even with millions of qubits.
